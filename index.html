Description:

The SCP Foundation is a global, clandestine organization committed to safeguarding humanity by containing and studying anomalous entities, objects, and phenomena that defy natural laws. Our mission is threefold: Secure, Contain, and Protect.

Secure: We identify and secure anomalous items and entities that pose a threat to normalcy. Our highly trained personnel operate under strict protocols to neutralize these threats, often working in hazardous and unpredictable environments.

Contain: Once secured, these anomalies are contained within our extensive network of secure facilities. Each SCP (Special Containment Procedure) is documented, with detailed instructions on how to keep the anomaly under control and prevent it from affecting the outside world. Containment is our top priority, as even a single breach can have catastrophic consequences.

Protect: By securing and containing these anomalies, we protect humanity from the potential dangers they pose. Our work ensures that the world continues to function normally, without the public ever knowing the threats that exist just out of sight. We also protect the anomalies themselves, maintaining ethical standards to the greatest extent possible, given the circumstances.

Operating in the shadows, the SCP Foundation remains committed to maintaining global security and stability. Our existence is classified, and the knowledge of our work is restricted to a need-to-know basis. We do what must be done to keep the world safe, often at great personal cost, because the alternative is unthinkable.
